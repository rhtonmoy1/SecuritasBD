All About Securitas Bangladesh Ltd.

Vigilance.Integrity.Protection
At Securitas Bangladesh Ltd, we stand firm on the pillars of vigilance, integrity, and protection. We are dedicated to fostering a safer environment for our clients and their customers. Through our specialized guarding services, we ensure unwavering surveillance and steadfast defense, prioritizing the security and well-being of all we serve.

  • Our Background

    • Established by Group Captain Dr. M Idris Ali in March 2004, our company initially operated under the name 'SECURITAS Security and Logistic Services Ltd' as a Partnership Organization, delivering comprehensive security and logistics solutions. Over time, we evolved into 'Securitas Bangladesh LTD,' a limited company led by three dedicated directors committed to transforming the landscape of security and logistics services in Bangladesh.

    Since our inception, we have consistently delivered our services with unwavering customer confidence. Our ethos revolves around achieving sustained growth in the security services sector. Presently, we boast a workforce of over 3000 security personnel deployed across Dhaka City and throughout Bangladesh, spanning even remote areas like Hatia & Shandip Islands. We take great pride in our track record, having served over 100 organizations, including offices, DOHS, banks, mobile companies, land development firms, garment factories, and various international aid organizations.

  • MISSION

    • Our mission is to safeguard individuals, businesses, and communities by providing cutting-edge security solutions, unwavering vigilance, and unparalleled expertise to create a secure environment.

  • VISION

    • To be the foremost leader in the security industry, driving innovation and setting the standard for excellence as we relentlessly pursue a safer world for our clients and their assets.

  • Our Values

    • SECURITAS's core values are summarized in three words:
    Integrity: Always acting according to sound, moral principles
    Vigilance: Observation and quick response to situations
    Helpfulness: Providing value-added service to our customers
