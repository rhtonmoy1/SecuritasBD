All About Securitas Bangladesh Ltd.

Since our founding, we’ve worked with numerous clients throughout the area. Great service begins and ends with experienced and friendly professionals, which is why we put so much consideration into selecting only the best to join our team. We complete projects efficiently and on schedule, and go above and beyond to form lasting relationships with our clients.

  • Our Background Securitas : A Short Profile

    • We initially established SECURITAS, as a Partnership Organization in March 2004 to provide Security & Logistic services. In November 2006 had been changed as “Security and Logistic Services Ltd”- a limited Company with 4 directors. In the same year, SECURITAS acquired a renowned Security company “Eagle Security Services”. Since the inception, we have been rendering our service with the utmost confidence of the customers. We believe in attaining a steady growth in the security services sector. As of today, we have more than 3000 security personnel deployed at Dhaka City and all over Bangladesh including Hatia & Shandip Islands. We do feel proud to announce that till today we have served more than 100 organizations including garments factories, Offices, DOHS, Banks, Mobile Companies, Land Development companies, and some International Aid organizations.

  • VISION OF SECURITAS

    • To build up a full range and integrated Security & Logistic business in Bangladesh and in abroad.

  • MISSION (AIM)

    • To carry out business on Security & Logistic Services in most reliable and professional manner.

  • STRATEGY

    • “Focusing on security by fostering an organization capable of providing specialized and added- value services for increasingly segmented markets and customer groups.”

  • Expansion

    • As of August 2008, SECURITAS has 3500 security personnel serving all around the country.

  • Regional Offices

    • SECURITAS has 06 regional offices strategically located in different parts of the country in order to respond quickly to clients needs.
